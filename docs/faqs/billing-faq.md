# Billing FAQ

**Applies to:** Workspace admins  
**Last updated:** 2026-01-03

## Overview
Common billing questions for TaskFlow workspaces.

## What plans are available?
Typical SaaS plans include:
- Free
- Pro
- Business

(Exact features depend on your product offering in this sample.)

## Where can I view invoices?
Go to `Settings > Workspace settings > Billing` and open **Invoices**.

## How do I change my plan?
1. Go to `Settings > Workspace settings > Billing`.
2. Select **Change plan**.
3. Select your plan and confirm.

## What happens if I cancel?
- Your workspace remains accessible until the end of the billing period.
- After the period ends, access may move to read-only (varies by plan).

## Do you offer refunds?
Refund policies vary. If you need help, contact support and include:
- Workspace name
- Invoice number
- Reason for request
